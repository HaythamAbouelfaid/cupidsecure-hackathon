Privacy & Security Center

We believe you shouldn't have to trade your privacy for safety. Here is how we protect your data.

How We Handle Your Conversations

In-Memory Only Processing

Your text and images are processed in temporary memory (RAM) and are discarded immediately after analysis. We do not store, log, or save your conversations to any database.

Automatic Anonymization

Our algorithms are designed to ignore personally identifiable information (PII) like real names, addresses, and phone numbers, focusing only on semantic patterns indicating fraud.

Secure Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our verification engine is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (banking-grade security).
