Frequently Asked Questions

Is CupidSecure free to use?
Yes! CupidSecure is currently free for all users. Our mission is to protect everyone from online romance scams regardless of their financial status.
How does the AI detection work?
We use advanced AI (Gemini 2.0 Flash) to analyze conversation patterns, language cues, and psychological triggers that are commonly found in known romance scams.
Is my data safe and private?
Absolutely. We process your data anonymously for analysis and do not store your personal conversations or images on our servers.
Can I report a scammer?
CupidSecure helps you identify scams. If you confirm a scam, we recommend reporting it to the platform you're using and relevant authorities like the FTC or FBI IC3.
How accurate is the AI detection?
Our Gemini-powered AI achieves 94% accuracy in identifying romance scam tactics, trained on thousands of real scam conversations and FTC case data.
Can I use CupidSecure on dating apps?
Yes! You can copy-paste conversations from any dating app, social media platform, or messaging service. CupidSecure analyzes the text regardless of where it came from.
What if someone I'm talking to gets flagged?
A flag doesn't mean they're definitely a scammer—it means patterns match known scam tactics. We provide detailed explanations so you can make informed decisions.
Is there a mobile app?
Currently CupidSecure is web-based and mobile-responsive. A dedicated mobile app is in development for iOS and Android, planned for Q2 2026.
